How it Works

Create Menus

After logging in on the platform, create menus that mirrors your paper ones that you've got in your restaurant.

Enhance entries

Add the right items inside each of the menus, and load whatever you like related to that entry: text descriptions, tags, ingredients, and multimedia material like photos and videos

After you load all the necessary pictures, our algorithm will create a 3D model that you can immediately see on your browser
